Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nalu Clean. Can't find your answer? Call us at (808) 555-0106.

Home › FAQ
What areas of Maui do you serve?
We serve all of Maui, including Kahului, Wailuku, Kihei, Wailea, Lahaina, Napili, Ka'anapali, Paia, Makawao, and Haiku. For remote areas in Upcountry or East Maui, please call us for availability.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide a key or key code. All our team members are background-checked and insured. You'll receive a notification when we arrive and when we're done, along with post-clean photos.
Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?
Yes, absolutely. We use only plant-based, non-toxic, biodegradable cleaning products. They are safe for keiki and pets, and certified safe for Hawaii's reef and water systems. We never use chlorine bleach, ammonia, or synthetic fragrances.
How do you price your services?
Pricing is based on service type and property size. Standard home cleans start at $120 for a 1-bedroom. Deep cleans start at $250. Vacation rental turnovers start at $150 per turnover. Commercial cleaning is custom-quoted. Book online for an instant estimate or call us.
How far in advance should I book?
For standard home cleaning, we recommend booking 2–3 days in advance. For deep cleans or move-in/out cleans, 3–5 days is ideal. For vacation rental turnovers with same-day needs, we offer priority scheduling for regular clients. During peak tourist season (November–March), book earlier as availability fills quickly.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a $50 cancellation fee. We understand life happens — if you have an emergency, just give us a call and we'll work with you.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?
Yes! We bring all cleaning products and equipment needed for every job — sprays, microfiber cloths, mops, vacuums, and scrubbers. For vacation rental turnovers, clients typically provide the guest amenities (shampoo, soap, etc.) that we restock, but we bring everything needed for the cleaning itself.
What's included in a vacation rental turnover?
Our standard vacation rental turnover includes: full cleaning of all rooms, changing linens (you provide fresh linens or we can arrange laundry), restocking amenities you've set aside, taking out trash, running the dishwasher, and a photo report sent to you confirming the property is guest-ready.
Are your team members background-checked?
Yes. Every Nalu Clean team member undergoes a full background check before being hired. They are also covered by our general liability insurance and bonded. We take your trust seriously — your home is in safe hands.
Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?
Absolutely. If you're not 100% satisfied with your clean, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the areas in question at no charge. Our goal is your complete satisfaction — we won't leave until you're happy.
