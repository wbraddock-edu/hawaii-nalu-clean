Founded 2019

Built From Maui, For Maui

Nalu Clean was founded by Kaimana and Leilani Kahananui, a husband-and-wife team from Wailuku who grew up watching their families care for their homes with pride. "Nalu" means "wave" in Hawaiian — reflecting both the island's spirit and the fresh, rolling energy we bring to every clean.

After years of frustration with cleaning services that used harsh chemicals — damaging to keiki, pets, and the reef systems they loved — Kaimana and Leilani made a commitment: professional-grade cleaning, zero compromises on safety.

Today, Nalu Clean is one of Maui's most trusted cleaning companies with a team of 12 certified, background-checked cleaners serving over 200 regular clients across the island.

Our Values
